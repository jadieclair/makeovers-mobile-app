Why Choose Us?

Welcome to Makeover Mobile App, your go-to destination for all your interior design needs.

We understand the importance of selecting the right designer for your project. That's why we have carefully vetted each designer in our network, ensuring that they possess the necessary qualifications, experience, and a proven track record of delivering outstanding results.

Classical Design

With meticulous attention to detail, our classical interior designers seamlessly blend ornate detailing, symmetrical layouts, and luxurious materials to transport you to a bygone era of elegance. Whether you desire the regal ambiance of a palace, the serenity of a Roman villa, or the timeless grace of a French château, our designers have the expertise to transform your vision into a reality.

Contemporary Design

Contemporary interior design embraces the essence of the present, reflecting the latest design trends and pushing boundaries to create spaces that are sleek, stylish, and effortlessly chic. Our designers have a keen eye for capturing the spirit of the times and translating it into captivating interiors that inspire and uplift.

Historical Design

With meticulous attention to detail, our historical interior designers capture the essence of each period, seamlessly incorporating architectural elements, furniture, and décor that transport you to another time. Whether you dream of an opulent Rococo salon, a cozy Tudor-style retreat, or the grandeur of an Art Deco masterpiece, our designers have the expertise and passion to bring your vision to life.

Our Services

Design

The art of design in interior design is a delicate balance of creativity, vision, and skill. It involves the thoughtful selection and arrangement of elements such as color, texture, pattern, and form to create visually stunning and harmonious spaces that evoke emotion, tell a story, and enhance the overall experience of those who inhabit them.

Architecture

Architecture plays a pivotal role in interior design, serving as the foundation for creating harmonious and functional spaces. It provides the framework that dictates the layout, flow, and structural elements, allowing interior designers to maximize the potential of a space while ensuring it aligns with the desired aesthetic and purpose.

Furnishing

Makeover Mobile designers are all professionals who are experienced and knowledgeable in the art of choosing pieces that complement the overall design style, harmonize with the space's color palette and textures, while also fulfilling the desired purpose and providing comfort for a well-balanced and cohesive interior.
